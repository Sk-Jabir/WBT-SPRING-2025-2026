My Project

Gym Management System

This project is a web-based system developed to simplify the management of gym operations. It helps gym staff manage member information, monitor attendance, and maintain payment records in an organized and efficient way.

Key Features
  • Member Management – Enables staff to register new members, update details, and manage plans.
  • Attendance Monitoring – Allows recording of member check-ins and tracking history.
  • Payment Handling – Supports managing fees and monitoring subscription status.

Supershop Management System

This project is a web-based system developed to simplify the management of supershop operations. It helps staff manage product information, monitor sales activities, and maintain customer records.

Key Features
  • Product Management – Allows staff to add products, update details, and track stock.
  • Sales Management – Enables recording of daily transactions and generating bills.
  • Inventory Tracking – Helps monitor stock levels and updates inventory automatically.
